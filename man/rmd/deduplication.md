- `id`: GTFS-realtime entity ID. These are required by the specification to be unique within a GTFS-realtime file, but sometimes are not. If there are non-unique IDs in the feed, they will be made unique when data are loaded by appending `_duplicate_1`, `_duplicate_2`, and so on and a warning will be issued, which guarantees that all rows from a single file have unique IDs. When working with archived data, there will quite likely be duplicated IDs between files archived at different times (path: `id` property of `FeedEntity` containing this object).
